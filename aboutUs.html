About Us

At Women Safety Map, we are committed to providing innovative solutions to ensure the safety and security of women everywhere. Our mission is to empower women by offering tools that allow them to stay informed and safe in their daily lives. With real-time alerts, safe route mapping, self-defense tips, and emergency assistance options, we strive to create a community where women can feel secure and confident.

Our platform leverages the latest technology to provide real-time updates on high-risk areas, safe zones, and much more. Whether you're traveling to a new area, walking home late at night, or just looking to stay informed, Women Safety Map has you covered.
